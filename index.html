Hi, I'm Kathryn.

I'm a software test engineer at the Space Telescope Science Institute, where I contribute to maintaining both automated and manual tests for applications that host telescope data. My role allows me to blend problem-solving with the field of astronomy, and I love being part of a team that supports cutting-edge discoveries about our universe.

I enjoy working with tools and technologies such as Robot Framework, FastAPI, Python, GitLab CI/CD pipelines, Selenium, and PostgreSQL- all of which help ensure that our systems are robust, efficient, and reliable.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about lifelong learning, progressive metal, getting lost in a good book, working towards my next big running goal, and striving to live an environmentally conscious lifestyle by avoiding animal products and supporting sustainable practices.

I created this website as a space to reignite my passion for coding and experiment with new ideas. It's a blend of personal reflection and creative exploration, where I share glimpses of the media I love, articles that inspire me, and the moments that make life meaningful. As a software test engineer and aspiring developer, this platform also serves as a canvas to relearn, grow, and experiment with web development while sharing a bit of myself.
